Instructions

 • To choose the type of match click on the buttons Single, Double or Team.
 • To choose the selection mode of the robots click on the checkbox Rand (for random choice) or Wght (the probability to be selected of two or more robots is proportional to the difference of their winning percentages: See the source code of JJArena.java for more details).
 • To play the matches in sequence without interruption click on the Loop checkbox (or uncheck it to exit loop mode).
 • To switch among the different view options check the boxes Scan, Trace, Track, Results or Graphs. Scan shows the radar scans, Trace shows the traces of the missiles, Track shows the paths of the robots, Results opens a window that shows the results of the matches (in a format compatible with "robots.txt") and, finally, Graph opens a windows that shows a graph of the winning percentages of a selected robot.
 • To change the speed of the simulation click x2 or /2. x2 increases the speed and /2 decrease it.
 • To change the update rate of the display click Smooth (60 fps), Fast (30 fps) or None (no update for faster tests).
 • To select the robots you want to see in the arena use the list in the top-right corner.
